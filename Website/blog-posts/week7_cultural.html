Meaningful work

I have been fortunate enough to perform a number of roles that have been directly impactful on both individuals and society as a whole.

In general, I try to seek out meaningful work. My approach has been to mix it up as sadly more meaningful work is not always available or viable.

I am often asked for advice on career direction and how to balance the pursuit of meaningful work with other career needs, such as skills, industry experience and improving networks.

I think that meaningful work is an ambition we all have, albeit are abilities to realise this for ourselves could be constrained by a whole number of factors that may or may not be within our control. I think this common ambition rises above stereotype threats (arising from being a minority in the workplace).
