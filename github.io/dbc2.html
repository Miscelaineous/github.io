London's Highlights!

The Sights

London is an old city and as such, has many great sights spanning over the centuries, dating from artifacts from Roman times, on show in the British Library, through to the latest award-winning architecture, such as The Shard - the tallest buildling in Europe.

Transport

London is a large city sprawling 35 by 27 miles. To facilitate movement around the city, London has the oldest tube network in the world, a infamous red bus network, cross-city train links, a light-railway that serves the East of London and access to the Eurostar, a train that travels through the Channel Tunnel into Europe.

Nightlife

London has a diverse and rich nightlife ranging from cultural activities such as film, theatre, museums and galleries through to thousands of bars, restaurants, nightclubs and casinos to entertain all possible interests, tastes and energy levels.
