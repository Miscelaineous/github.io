What problem does the BBC News website aim to solve?

The BBC News website aims to provide up-to-date news across a diverse range of topics, including current affairs news, from around the world.

In doing so, the BBC News website attempt to smartly fragment the news it has generated and is communicating into clear content boxes, labelled clearly and simply, to aid the user in finding the information they seek, from a huge breadth of information available, in an easy to visualise and access manner.
